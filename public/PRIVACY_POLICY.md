# Privacy Policy for Chromoscientia

**Last updated:** March 2025

This privacy policy describes how Chromoscientia (“we”, “the app”) handles information when you use our mobile and web applications.

---

## 1. What data we collect and why

When you use Chromoscientia, we collect and process the following only to provide the game:

- **Nickname** – The display name you choose when creating or joining a room. It is visible only to other players in the same room during that game session.
- **Game and room data** – Room codes, player list, in-game color descriptions, color guesses, and scores. This is used to run the multiplayer game and show results.
- **Device-generated identifier** – An anonymous player ID generated and stored on your device (and sent to our servers only for the room you are in). We do not use it to identify you personally.

We do **not** collect your name, email, phone number, or other contact information. We do **not** require an account to play.

---

## 2. Where data is stored

Game and room data (including nicknames and in-game content) is stored on servers operated by **Google Firebase** (Firestore) to sync the game between players. Our use of Firebase is governed by [Google’s privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). Data is processed in connection with providing the app only.

---

## 3. Data retention and deletion

**Data, including nicknames, is deleted after the game ends.**

- When a game or room ends, the data for that room and game (including all nicknames, descriptions, guesses, and scores) is no longer needed for gameplay.
- We automatically delete old room and game data so that it is not kept long-term. Typically, data is removed when the room closes or within a short time afterward (within hours).
- We do not retain your nickname or other game data after the session is over.

**Data on your device:** The anonymous player ID is stored only on your device (e.g. in local storage). You can clear it by clearing the app’s data or uninstalling the app.

---

## 4. Sharing of data

We do **not** sell your data. We do **not** share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing.

Data you provide (such as your nickname and in-game text) is shared only as necessary to run the game—for example, with other players in the same room during that session. This is inherent to how the multiplayer game works.

---

## 5. User-generated content

You may type short descriptions or make guesses during the game. This content is used only for the game and is deleted as described above. Please do not submit offensive or harmful content. We do not monitor content in real time but may take action if we become aware of misuse.

---

## 6. Children

Chromoscientia is a casual game that does not target children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will work to delete it.

---

## 7. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

---

## 8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, you can contact us at the support channel or email you provide in your app store listing or website.
